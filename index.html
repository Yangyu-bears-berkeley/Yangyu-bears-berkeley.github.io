About me

 

I am currently a postdoc with Berkeley Education Alliance for Resaerch in Singapore (BEARS), University of California, Berkeley, supervised by Prof. Costas J. Spanos (UC Berkeley) and Prof. Guoqiang Hu (Nanyang Technological University). Prior to that I got my PhD. from Tsinghua University under the guidance of Prof. Xiaohong Guan and Prof. (Samual) Qing-Shan Jia.

My research interests are in the area of cyber-physical energy systems (CPES), such as smart grid and smart buildings from the perspective of market mechanism design, optimization and control. The goal is to advance a reliable, flexible and sustainable CPES with integrated interdisciplinary theories and techniques such as decentralized control, reinforcement learning (RL), game theory and market mechanisms. Beyond that I am also excited about developing foundational theories and techniques by exploring and exploiting the systems structures and features of CPES, such as decentralized optimization for structured non-convex problems , that can lead to computational efficient solutions to the counterparts in various realms.

Research Interest

During my Ph.D. study and my first year career as a research fellow, I mainly focus on optimization-based control methods for Large-scale dynamic systems with application to smart buildings, smart grids and cyber physical systems including

  1. Decentralize or distributed optimization, decentralized decision-making, game

  2. Event-based optimization

  3. Stochastic optimization, Markov decision process, reinforcement learning

  4. Data-driven analysis and control of complex dynamic systems

In the last five decades, I have worked in a number of different areas.
